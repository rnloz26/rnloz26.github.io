About Me

I am an applied mathematician and computational researcher specializing in nonlinear dynamical systems, control theory, and biomolecular network modeling. My work focuses on understanding and designing biological systems through mechanistic modeling and dynamical analysis.

I am particularly interested in the control of gene regulatory networks, emergent behaviors such as oscillations and pulse generation, and the integration of control theory with synthetic biology.
